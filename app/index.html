Services we offer in the Kelowna area

Beginning with free consultations and quotes, we help you plan your project according to your budget. Our team of in-house carpenters demolish, build or renovate your structure according to all local building codes and standards.

Our subtrade affiliates install all the electrical, plumbing, mechanical, insulation, drywall, and tile that make your home function at the highest level of efficiency and comfort possible.

Our crew of finishers take care of all the final touches, from doors and trim to cabinets. We customize your finishes to your particular taste.

We'll be the first to arrive at your home and the last to leave, overseeing the installation and execution of your entire project from start to finish.
